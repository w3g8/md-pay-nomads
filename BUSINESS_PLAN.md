# Pay Nomads — Business Plan

## Mission
Travel money without the fees. The all-in-one financial and travel services platform for digital nomads — wallet, payments, visa services, and document management.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. FX Spread (Core)
- **0.5% transparent FX markup** on all currency conversions
- Mid-market rate + 0.5% — undercuts banks (3-5%), Wise (0.35-1.5%), Revolut (weekend surcharges)
- **Projected:** €5 per €1,000 spend → €60/yr per active user

### 2. QR Payment Processing Fees
- **0.3-0.8% merchant commission** on QR Ph P2M, PromptPay, QRIS, PIX settlements
- Merchants pay, users pay nothing
- **Projected:** $0.15 per transaction avg → $18/yr per active user

### 3. Passport & Visa Renewal Services ⭐ NEW
- **Passport renewal concierge** — €149-299 per renewal
  - Document preparation, photo compliance check, appointment booking
  - Embassy liaison, expedited processing where available
  - 10+ countries supported: UK, US, Ireland, Australia, Canada, EU members
  - In-app document upload via SecureVault (encrypted, zero-knowledge)
  - Status tracking with push notifications
- **Visa application assistance** — €99-199 per application
  - Digital Nomad Visa applications for 51+ countries
  - Document checklist generator per nationality × destination
  - Income verification letter generation (from wallet transaction history)
  - Health insurance certificate bundling (SafetyWing partnership)
- **Visa extension reminders** — free (drives engagement)
  - Track days in-country, alert before overstay
  - Suggest visa run destinations with cheapest flights
- **Revenue model:**
  - Service fee: €149-299 per passport renewal
  - Visa application fee: €99-199 per application
  - Affiliate commission from travel insurance (15-25% of premium)
  - **Projected:** €75/yr per user (30% conversion on renewals)

## Passport & Visa Renewal — Detailed Service Design

### Passport Renewal Flow (In-App)
1. **Select nationality** → show requirements, processing time, fee
2. **Upload documents** via SecureVault (encrypted):
   - Current passport scan (OCR extracts data automatically)
   - New passport photo (AI compliance check: background, face size, expression)
   - Proof of address / supporting documents
3. **Pay service fee** from wallet (€149-299 depending on country & speed)
4. **We handle:**
   - Form completion & submission
   - Embassy/consulate appointment booking (where applicable)
   - Courier coordination for document delivery
   - Status tracking with real-time updates
5. **Delivery:** new passport shipped to user's current location worldwide
